Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad, then select SleepyBaby to manage or cancel your subscription.

The Listen feature isn't working. What should I do?

Make sure you've granted microphone permission to SleepyBaby. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and ensure SleepyBaby is enabled. Also check that your device isn't in Silent mode.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All sounds are stored locally on your device. Once downloaded, SleepyBaby works without an internet connection.

Is SleepyBaby safe to use with my baby?

SleepyBaby is designed as a sleep aid using gentle sounds. However, please note that it is not a baby monitor and should not replace adult supervision. Always follow safe sleep guidelines recommended by your pediatrician.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase you'd like to request a refund for.

I have a feature suggestion. How can I share it?

We'd love to hear from you! Send your ideas to sleepy_baby7r@icloud.com and we'll consider them for future updates.
